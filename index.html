Welcome to the Nakota Clan

In the harsh lands of 1899, the Nakota people stand as guardians of tradition, honor, and the old ways. We are warriors, hunters, and keepers of sacred wisdom passed down through generations.

Who We Are

The Nakota Clan is a tight-knit community on the Royal 1899 Georgian server, dedicated to authentic roleplay and preserving Native American culture within Red Dead Redemption 2. We value respect, loyalty, and the bonds of brotherhood.

Our Mission

  • Protect our lands and people from those who would do us harm
  • Preserve the traditions and wisdom of our ancestors
  • Stand together as one unified tribe against injustice
  • Live in harmony with nature and honor the Great Spirit

Clan Lore & History

The Beginning

Long before the white man's iron horses scarred the earth, the Nakota people roamed these lands freely. We are descendants of the great Sioux nations, bound by blood and tradition. Our ancestors hunted the buffalo, spoke with the spirits, and lived as one with the land.

The Changing Times

As the year 1899 draws to a close, our people face threats unlike any before. The government seeks to confine us to reservations, settlers encroach upon our sacred grounds, and the old ways are under siege. But the Nakota do not break easily.

Our Territory

We claim the northern territories as our homeland, where the mountains meet the plains and the rivers run clear. Here, we hunt, trade, and defend our way of life. Our camps are places of gathering, where stories are shared around the fire and the old songs still echo.

Sacred Beliefs

The Nakota hold sacred the balance between earth and sky, man and beast. We seek guidance from the Great Spirit and honor our ancestors through ceremony and deed. The eagle feather represents courage, the wolf represents loyalty, and the bear represents strength. These symbols guide our path.

Tribal Code & Laws

The Sacred Rules

  • Loyalty Above All: Never betray your brothers and sisters. The tribe comes before self.
  • Honor Your Word: A Nakota's word is their bond. Speak truth and keep promises.
  • Protect the Weak: Children, elders, and those who cannot fight are under our protection.
  • Respect the Land: Take only what you need. Waste nothing. Honor the spirits of all creatures.
  • Answer to Authority: Obey the Chief and War Chief in all matters. Question in private, support in public.

Rules of Combat

  • Never attack a fellow clan member except in training or ceremony
  • Show mercy to those who surrender, unless they have committed grave offenses
  • Always assist a clan member in danger, regardless of personal risk
  • Take trophies only from worthy opponents
  • Avenge fallen brothers and sisters

Consequences

Those who break the sacred code face judgment from the Chief and Elder Council. Punishments may include loss of rank, exile, or in extreme cases, death. We are merciful but firm. The clan's survival depends on unity and discipline.
